    Frequently Asked Questions

    How long does shipping take?

    Orders are typically processed within 1–2 business days. Delivery usually takes 3–7 business days depending on your location.

    Can I track my order?

    Yes. Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email to monitor your delivery in real time.

    What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept major credit and debit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment options available at checkout.

    Can I cancel or modify my order?

    Orders can be modified or canceled within 12 hours of purchase. After that, the order may already be processed for shipping.

    What if my product arrives damaged?

    If your item arrives damaged, please contact our support team within 48 hours with photos, and we’ll arrange a replacement or refund.

    Warranty Policy

    1-Year Limited Warranty

    All products purchased from our store are covered by a 1-year limited warranty against manufacturing defects.

    What Is Covered?

    • Manufacturing defects
    • Hardware malfunctions under normal use
    • Audio or connectivity failures

    What Is Not Covered?

    • Physical damage caused by misuse
    • Water damage (unless product is rated waterproof)
    • Normal wear and tear

    How to Claim Warranty

    To start a warranty claim, contact our support team with your order number and a description of the issue. Our team will guide you through the next steps.

    Returns & Refunds

    We offer a 14-day return policy. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return the item within 14 days of delivery.

    • Item must be unused and in original packaging
    • Proof of purchase is required
    • Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after inspection
